This App Is More Secure
Than You Think.

Secure Vault does not use biometric authentication — fingerprint or face unlock can be forced by others. Instead, your vault is protected by a master PIN or pattern that only you know. Without it, no one — not even a hacker with physical access to your device — can see what's inside.

✅ No Biometrics ✅ No Cloud Upload ✅ No Server Access ✅ Data Encrypted Locally ✅ No One Can Peek

All your files, passwords, and notes live inside an encrypted local vault on your device. The storage is coded in a way that even if someone connects your phone to a computer, they cannot read or extract any data from Secure Vault. Your privacy is truly yours.

📱 Switching Phones?

Your Vault. Moves With You.

You never have to start over. Transfer all your private data to a new phone — securely and instantly.

1

Create a Backup File

Go to App Settings in Secure Vault and tap "Download Backup File". A single encrypted file containing all your data will be saved to your phone.

→
2

Send It to Your New Phone

Share the backup file via WhatsApp, Bluetooth, USB, or any transfer method. The file looks like gibberish to anyone who opens it — all data is stored in encrypted code, invisible to all apps and people.

→
3

Restore on New Phone

Open Secure Vault on your new phone. On the first screen, you'll see a "Restore" option. Select your backup file and enter your master password. All your photos, videos, passwords, and notes will be fully recovered — exactly as you left them.

🔐

Hacker-proof transfer: The backup file cannot be opened by any other app, file manager, or person. Even if intercepted, the data inside is fully encrypted and meaningless without your master password. Only Secure Vault can read it — and only after you authenticate.
