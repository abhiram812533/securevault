# securevault
Securely hide photos, videos, passwords and notes with strong encryption.
Secure Vault is a privacy protection app designed to securely store and protect your photos, videos, passwords, and private notes. The app provides a secure vault inside your device where all your sensitive data is protected with encryption and secure authentication.
Privacy and Security
Secure Vault is built with privacy as the top priority. All data is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to any server or cloud. Only you can access your vault.
Security features include:
Local encrypted storage
PIN lock protection
Pattern lock protection
Screenshot and screen recording protection
App preview hidden in recent apps
Offline access with no internet required
Your data stays only on your device and remains fully under your control.
